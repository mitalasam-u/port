About Us

Cash for Growth Finance Limited is a Licensed Financial entity founded in 2020, specializing in providing financial empowerment through the provision of secured loans, Sacco loans, and commercial loans. We have a focused approach to ensuring that the quality of service provided to customers is extremely high and that the accuracy of the underwriting and processing is second to none.

We believe that if we can empower business owners to be successful, they will go on to make a meaningful impact on the communities we are all part of.


Our Vision

To fuel ambition and social development in the community.


Our Goals

Build a connected financial experience by empowering business owners, salary earners with the financial tools they need to realize their plans full potential
